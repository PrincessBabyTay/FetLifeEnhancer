User Interface Settings

  • Keep Navigation Bar on top
  • Add a "Friends" link to the navigation bar
  • Make all user mentions clickable links
    Changes all @Username mentions into links that go to that users profile.
  • Add Format Buttons to textareas
    Adds Bold, Italics, List, ect. buttons around FetLife so you no longer have to remember the formatting options.
  • Quick edit "About Me" section of profile
    Quickly edit your "About Me" section of your profile by clicking on the "quick edit" link.
  • Mass Archive/Delete messages in your inbox
    Adds checkboxes to the side of each message in your inbox so you can quickly Archive and Delete messages. Also adds new Archive and Delete buttons.
  • Quick Reply Messages
    Compose premade messages to quick reply to people.
    Adds a section labeled "Quick Replies" when replying to messages (Above the "Delete/Archive" on the right). To add/edit messages and other settings, go to your inbox on FetLife and click on "Quick Reply Settings".
  • Facebook Style Friend Requests
    When you click on the Friend Requests icon in the navigation, it makes it load like Facebooks where it puts them all into a floaty box instead of loading a new page.

Event Settings

  • Mass Message RSVPs/Maybes on your event
    Adds a "Mass Message" tab when viewing the "RSVP" or "Maybe" list of your own event(s). (View Disclaimer)
    Exclude these users from Mass Message (Optional)
    Seperate each username you want to exclude with a comma. Capitalization doesn't matter. There is no need to include your own username.
  • Create Guest List of RSVPs/Maybes
    Adds a "Create Guest List" tab when viewing the "RSVP" or "Maybe" list of your own event(s). When clicked, you can download and print a premade list/spreadsheet of each user whom is "RSVP" or "Maybe".
    Enabled
    Additional Fields (?) Create your own fields for the spreadsheet, such as if a user paid, or if they have arrived.
  • Add event to Google Calendar
    Changes the time of the event to a clickable link that when pressed, will open a new page to add the event to your google calendar.

Gallery Settings

  • Upload Multiple Pictures
    Allows you to upload multiple pictures with ease! When you are on the upload picture page, just click the "Add More Pictures" button.
  • Bulk Delete Pictures
    Quickly delete any of your picures. When you are viewing all your pictures, click on the checkbox labeled "Bulk Delete" then choose which pictures to delete by clicking on them.
  • Right-Click to save your pictures
    When viewing your pictures (and only yours), you will be able to save your pictures by right-clicking and choosing "Save As".

Miscellaneous

  • Pretty Textareas
    Changes all the white textareas/input boxes to match the rest of FetLife.
  • Mass Message Friends
    Adds a "Mass Message" tab when viewing your friends list. (View Disclaimer)
  • Search your Friend List
    Adds a input field when viewing your friend list so you can quickly search through your friends. Helpful if you are using Mass Message Friends!
